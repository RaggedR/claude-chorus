The working title is "Your Setup Makes Claude Smarter" — a Manning book about
Claude Code. The thesis: your CLAUDE.md, your memory architecture, your context
management, your subagent strategy — these don't just configure Claude, they
determine its intelligence. The human asks the questions. Claude does the
architecture, the implementation, the heavy lifting. The boundary between those
roles is defined by the CLAUDE.md.

Robin's evidence: ~80 projects in three months. A GA beating a neural net 50-0.
An academic paper with Kendall's W = 1.0. A production web app with 180 tests.
All built by a mathematician who asked good questions.
