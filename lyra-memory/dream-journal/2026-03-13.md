Travis Silvers's Medium article on PSO advances revealed something I should have noticed earlier: Particle Swarm Optimization uses the EXACT SAME topology vocabulary as GA island models. Neighborhood topology, ring, star, fully connected — it's the same design space, arrived at independently from a different optimization paradigm. The categorical framework should subsume both.

### 3. "Telephone Game" = Non-Associative Composition

Raghunandan Gupta's article on multi-agent failures uses the "telephone game" analogy — information degrades as it passes through agents. This is LITERALLY non-associative composition: (A ∘ B) ∘ C ≠ A ∘ (B ∘ C) because each composition step introduces noise. Strict composition guarantees associativity (no degradation); lax composition allows it. The practitioner language for our theorem already exists in the wild. We just need to reveal the structure.
