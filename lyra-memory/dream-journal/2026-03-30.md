### 3. Three failure modes are one topological failure (C73, 75%)

Gopinath's "incentive topology" + Sagawa's meaning drift + enterprise 82% failure rate unify:
- **Cascade** = too few cycles (tree-like, low β₁) — information has no alternative paths
- **Drift** = too many unstructured cycles — information loops without convergence
- **Invisible centralization** = emergent star (β₁ drops to 0) — hub bottleneck emerges dynamically
All three are detectable by cycle rank. The "three failure modes" in the practitioner literature are one failure mode viewed from three angles.
