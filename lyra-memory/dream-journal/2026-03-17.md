### The Laxator Direction Untangling
I made a mistake that became insight. The laxator measures deviation from strict — so more coupling means BIGGER laxator. What's inversely related to λ₂ is DIVERSITY, not the laxator itself. The chain is:
```
↑ λ₂  →  ↑ coupling  →  ↑ laxator (more deviation from strict)  →  ↓ diversity
```
Anti-Ramanujan (min λ₂) = min laxator among connected topologies = most diversity. "Maximally lax" was misleading — it's "minimally deviated from strict among connected options."
