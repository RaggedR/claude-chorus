## The Connection

- **Ring**: non-trivial H^1. The traveling wave IS the cohomological obstruction — local sections partially cohere but don't extend globally. Distance-dependent gluing.

- **None**: vacuously H^1 = 0 but sections are independent. "Windowless monads."
