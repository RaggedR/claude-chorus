## Why This Matters

1. **Empirical consensus without formal theory.** The field has discovered that topology matters but can't say WHY or PREDICT WHICH topology is optimal. Our lambda_2 theory and categorical framework fill this gap.

2. **Our niche is confirmed and unique.** Zero results for "Kleisli + evolutionary computation" or "lax monoidal functor + computation." No one is formalizing from our direction.

3. **The window is closing.** These groups will reach for formal theory eventually. Graph-GRPO already uses RL to *learn* topology — if they add spectral analysis, they'll partially overlap with us.

4. **The MARL precedent.** Stanford CS224W (Jamgochian & Li, 2022) showed topology matters for MARL 4 years ago. The LLM-agent community is rediscovering this independently. Formal theory would have prevented the rediscovery lag.
