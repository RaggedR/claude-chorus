## The Connection
WL graph isomorphism testing can be expressed as a functor from the category of graphs to a category of color refinements. The key insight: the power of a GNN (graph neural network) is bounded by the WL hierarchy — and this hierarchy is a functorial one.

For evolutionary computation: if population topology is a graph, and the WL functor determines what structural properties are visible to information flow, then the WL hierarchy constrains what a topology CAN express about diversity dynamics. Higher WL dimensions = finer topological distinctions = more precise diversity control.
