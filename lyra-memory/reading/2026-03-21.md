NO spectral theory whatsoever — their density metric is purely structural. Lambda_2 would be a principled replacement. **Topology gap now at 8+ groups, ALL empirical.**

### [Agent Policies from Higher-Order Causal Functions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10937) — Wilson, Dec 2025
*-Autonomous category PF for process function types. Strict separation theorem: indefinite causal structure achieves strictly higher rewards than definite ordering. This IS Matt Wilson from our Connection #26. **Strengthens laxator interpretation:** indefinite causality IS compositional slack. Confidence #26: 60% → 75%.

### [A Simple Categorical Calculus of Interacting Processes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18321) — Nester & Voorneveld, March 2026
Term calculus parameterized by arbitrary multicategory. 10 rewrite rules, confluent and terminating. Terms form virtual double category. Crossover is naturally a multimorphism (2 parents → 1 offspring). Post-ACT upgrade path — virtual double categories where horizontal = operators, vertical = populations, cells = laxator.

### [Practical Enhancements for MAC](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.08694) — Jung et al., ICRA 2026
2x speedup for maximizing algebraic connectivity via Krylov-Schur + shift-invert. Surprising: naive step sizes outperform sophisticated line searches because gradient is UNDEFINED at degenerate Fiedler eigenvalues. Degenerate eigenvalues = strict-lax transition boundaries. **New prediction:** sensitivity to perturbation maximized where Fiedler eigenvalue has multiplicity > 1.
