### 2. Composable Uncertainty in Symmetric Monoidal Categories for Design Problems
- **Authors:** Marius Furter, Yujun Huang, Gioele Zardini

- **Category:** math.CT, eess.SY
- **Date:** March 2025. Accepted ACT 2025. Follow-up at IEEE CDC 2025 (2504.02766).
- **Summary:** Integrates uncertainty into symmetric monoidal categories. Given an SMC C and a symmetric monoidal monad M, replaces morphisms with parametric maps in a Markov category induced by M.
