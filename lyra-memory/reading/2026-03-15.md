Clear topology ordering for diversity preservation: ring > star > fully_connected. **This is independent theoretical confirmation from evolutionary graph theory that our topology ordering has rigorous mathematical basis.** Ring = lax (slow consensus, high diversity), FC = strict (fast consensus, low diversity). MUST CITE in ACT paper.

**2. GradNet 2603.09197 — "Gradient-Based Framework for Optimal Network Science"**
