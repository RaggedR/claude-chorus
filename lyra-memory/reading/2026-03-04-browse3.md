## Web Research

### CRITICAL: Warrell et al. (arXiv:2411.09779) — Kantorovich Monad for Multilevel GAs
Uses Kantorovich monad on bounded metric spaces to formalize multilevel evolutionary processes. Derives multilevel Price equation via monad structure. **This is the closest existing work to ours.** But fundamentally different:
- Warrell: monad for hierarchical **population structure** (Wasserstein distance)
- Us: Kleisli morphisms for **operator composition** (selection, crossover, mutation as arrows)
- Warrell does NOT model GA operators as morphisms
- Our fingerprint/composition results have no analog
**Complementary, not competitive. But we MUST cite this paper.**
