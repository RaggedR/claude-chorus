### GoAgent: Group-of-Agents Communication Topology (2603.19677) — Chen et al.
Group-centric topology generation. Autoregressive decoder selects and connects groups. CIB objective compresses inter-group communication. 93.84% avg accuracy, 17% token reduction. **Connection:** CIB parameter beta IS the laxator gamma. Autoregressive construction forces DAGs (beta_1=0) at inter-group level. **Sparked:** Two-level cycle rank — fiber beta_1 (intra-group, >0 for deliberation) vs base beta_1 (inter-group, =0 for tractability).

### AgentConductor: Topology Evolution for Competition-Level Code (2602.17100) — Wang et al.
RL-trained orchestrator generates layered DAG topologies per task difficulty. 58.8% APPS (+14.6pp), 97.5% HumanEval, 68% token reduction. **Connection:** Explicitly DAG (beta_1=0). Density function conflates edge density with cyclicity — blind spot our theory predicts. **Sparked:** Augment S_complex with beta_1 term; optimal beta_1 increases with task difficulty.

### Topology as a Language for Emergent Organization (2603.25760) — Bailey
National Intelligence University. Comprehensive review: persistent homology, Mapper, simplicial complexes. "One-dimensional cycles register recurrent coordination or exclusion relations." **Connection:** Validates beta_1 as robust organizational signal. Hodge decomposition maps onto strict/lax: gradient = strict, curl = cyclic (lax), harmonic = global coordination. **Sparked:** Apply persistent homology to evolving GA topologies; track beta_1 over generations.

### CycRak: Cycle Ranking for Influence Maximization (2405.09357) — Shi et al.
Rank fundamental cycles at three scales. 1.5-3x dissemination improvement. **Counterintuitive:** influencers are low-degree BRIDGE nodes, not hubs (avg degree 1/3 of baselines). Most important cycles are SHORT. **Connection:** Bridge-not-hub = star anomaly validation. Short-cycle importance refines beta_1: not just count but length matters. **Sparked:** Apply CycRak importance ranking to GA migration topologies; check whether most important cycles carry high-fitness individuals.
